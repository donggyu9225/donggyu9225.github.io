Description

Mr. Tony Nguyen is a new teacher at Saint Paul. Coming from a varied and multifaceted background, he brings a breadth of knowledge and experience for the students. Even though he holds a degree in Mathematics from UC San Diego, the art and humanties have always been an interest of his. An avid reader, Tony is interested in the interaction between culture, art, and technology, and what that means for the lives of ordinary people. He has received his training from the California State University at Long Beach, where he enrolled in their credentialing program for Art Teachers. Drawing and painting continue to be a lifelong hobby of his.
