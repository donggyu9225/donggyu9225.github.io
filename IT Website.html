⏰ Time Management Skills ⏱️

Why is it important to use time efficiently?

It's very important to use time efficiently, especially as you get older. As we get older, there are more things that we need to do, and there will be a lot more homework or other works to do. Therefore, it's important to plan out our works in prior to have the best work efficiency, and possibly have some free time for ourselves, too.

©Bianca Barratt - Forbes - How To Write A To Do List That You'll Actually Stick To

How do we use our time efficiently?

  1. Make a list of things you usually do in a normal day.
  2. Divide it into things that you must do and things you want to do.
  3. Prioritize your must-do things based on their due dates.
  4. Do the must-do things in an order that you created in Step 3.
  5. After finishing all the must-do things, start doing the things you want to do in your free time!
©CFI Team - CFI - A Brief Guide to Time Management

Materials Needed

  • Clock ⏱️🕰️
  • Journal for the To Do List 📒📓
  • Calendar to track your achivements 🗓️📆

How to evaluate your progress

  • ✅ Check if you were able to turn in all the works on time
  • ✅ Check if were able to have enough time left even after finishing all the required works
  • ✅ Check with supervisors like parents to manage your time and plan more efficiently

Conclusion

If you follow all of these steps, you will be able to manage your time efficiently and finish all your works on time. Time-management skill is very useful thing in life, so it's important to learn it as fast as possible.
